## 💡 Study Recommendations
1. **Vocabulary First**: Learn vocabulary with audio or flashcards before starting the lesson.
2. **Grammar & Sentence Patterns (文型)**: Read the grammar explanations carefully and link each pattern to its grammar note.
3. **Practice A, B, C (練習A, B, C)**: Speak out loud and write your answers down.
